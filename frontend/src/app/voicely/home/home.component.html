Problems
  • We can understand the English language very well while when it comes to speaking or interaction, almost 50% of us make grammatical Mistakes.

  • Doing grammatical mistakes in professional communications leads to a bad impact and ends up with low confidence of speaker.

  • Students don't have adequate resources to improve their grammatical or pronunciation mistakes in real-time while speaking.

  • There is no application which checks your verbal communication skills and corrects you or gives you feedback.
